Yuriko Deck V4

Strategies

  • If you have black marked connection and skyllclamp, you can keep feeding tokens into battle and they'll either win or you get to draw two cards.

  • You can attack with Changeling Outcast that can't be blocked and then use ninjitsu to bring in another ninja.

  • pretty sure one of the ninjas can put another ninja back in the deck which means you could keep cycling them with the skeleton creature if they have a good enters battlefield feature.

  • Get Sink into Stupor in the deck which can be played as a land if you need one or bounce a nonland permanent opponent card.

  • Can attach with something and use Vodalian Illusionist to phase out a defender. Or, phase out an attacking creature?

  • Use feed the swarm and reanimate to kill a creature and then take control of it immediately. Same could be done with Infernal Grasp.

  • Tragic Slip - an instant for 1 black you can use to give a creature -13/-13 if something died on the turn.

  • Don't use crippling chill on your turn. wait until an opponent has done their untap step. The text says it won't untap during the next one so you get to knock something out for two turns if I'm reading this right.

  • If you have Shizo, Death's Storehouse, use a black to tap it so a creature can attack with fear. (make sure there aren't artifact or other black creatures that can counter it.

  • use secret tunnel with 4 mana to have two creatures that can't be blocked on the turn.
